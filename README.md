## Endloader 3300 Series

The International Harvester 3300 (specifically the 3300B model) is a vintage compact skid-steer loader featuring a Wisconsin air-cooled gas engine, 4-wheel drive, and two-lever skid steering. [1, 2, 3]  
Engine & Performance 

• Engine: Wisconsin 4-cylinder, air-cooled gas engine (often the VG4D model) 
• Horsepower: Approximately 37 HP 
• Drive: Hydrostatic drive with infinitely variable forward and reverse speeds from 0 to 8 mph 
• Traction: 4-wheel drive with oscillating axles to keep wheels on the ground and reduce bounce [1, 2, 3]  

Dimensions & Handling 

• Steering: 2-lever skid steering allowing 360-degree turns within its own track 
• Standard Bucket Width: Roughly 55 inches 
• Tires: Typically equipped with 9.50-16.5 or similar utility/flotation tires 
• Tread Widths: Changeable tread widths via reversible wheels [1, 2, 4]  

Features 

• Safety: Roll-Over Protective Structure (ROPS) 
• Hydraulics: Fast-cycling loader system with universal manual quick-attach options available for various vintage attachments [1, 2]
